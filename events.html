Before you book

Koshiyali Launch Tasting — ticket FAQ

Answers for guests booking the Koshiyali Launch Tasting — read event details above, then secure checkout.

What does the £20 ticket include?

Entry to the Koshiyali Launch Tasting on 18 July 2026 (doors 6pm), a welcome pour, tasting flight of Koshi Chhyang chhyang, and access to music and open pours until last pour at 10pm. Food pairing is planned and confirmed with the venue.

Is my ticket refundable if the venue changes?

If we move to a different Folkestone venue, your ticket remains valid and we will email all ticket holders with the new address. If the event is cancelled, we will contact you directly about refunds or transfers. For other requests, email info@koshichhyang.com.

Are there ID requirements?

Yes. This is an 18+ alcohol event in the UK. Please bring valid photo ID (passport or driving licence). We may check ID at the door.

Is the venue accessible?

We are confirming step-free access, toilets, and nearest parking with the venue. Once announced, accessibility details will be included in the venue email to all ticket holders and on this page. Contact us in advance if you have specific needs.
