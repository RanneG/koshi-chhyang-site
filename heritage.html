Our story

Why we brought Koshi Chhyang to the market.

Koshi Chhyang is a UK-based Nepalese rice wine business on a mission to introduce the world to the flavours of authentic rice wine. Our pre-launch preparations are a testament to our commitment to quality and tradition.
